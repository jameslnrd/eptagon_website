About

Eptagon is an artistic collective founded in Grenoble (France) in 2016. It houses projets ranging from extreme metal bands to digital art.

Eptagon's artists thrive on experimentation, mixed forms, and collective emergence.

The collective is now composed of around twenty musicians, videographers, visual artists, sound engineers and programmers who craft their creations and network within this framework, through new collaborations, exchanges and encounters.

Information

Eptagon is currently a long-term resident at Le Ciel in Grenoble.

Eptagon works in close collaboration with Plastic Lobster Studios.

Eptagon regularly collaborates with ARCAN, organisers of the DN[A] digital arts festival.

Eptagon is an association under the French law of 1901.
