About

Eptagon is an artistic collective founded in Grenoble (France) in 2016, home to projets ranging from metal bands to digital art.

Eptagon's artists thrive on experimentation, mixed forms, and collective emergence.

The collective is now composed of around twenty musicians, video makers, visual artists, sound engineers and programmers who develop their creations and network within this framework, through new collaborations, exchanges and encounters.
